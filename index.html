About GreenSpark

GreenSpark is a sustainability-focused business concept centered on helping people, communities, and small organizations adopt practical, affordable, and measurable sustainable behaviors.

Journal overview

Continuous Improvement

The work reflects an ongoing process of learning, refining, and strengthening sustainability strategy over time.
